## Farm narrative

The Southern California case study is organized into six stages:

1. Read the land
2. Shape the site
3. Build the plant pipeline
4. Experiment and teach
5. Plant together
6. Watch it take shape

No exact project dates are stated because they were not supplied. This avoids inventing a timeline while still showing chronological progress.
